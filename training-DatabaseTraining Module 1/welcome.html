Our Example Project

Throughout this training, we'll build a petroleum sales database for a fictional fuel distributor. This practical example will demonstrate real-world database applications while teaching fundamental concepts.

Petroleum Sales Database

Our example database will track:

  • Customers who purchase fuel
  • Products (different types of fuel and lubricants)
  • Sales transactions
  • Delivery drivers
  • Delivery records

This data model allows us to answer business questions like:

  • Which products are most profitable?
  • Who are our top customers?
  • How efficient are our delivery drivers?
